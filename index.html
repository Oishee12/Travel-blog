My Travel Blog

Most people think travel means beaches or mountains—but my November trip was all about industry and history, and it was surprisingly fascinating!
Last November, our class went on an industrial tour. Our first stop was Walton Hi-Tech Industry PLC, followed by visits to a historic Jamiderbari (landlord house).
Walking into the Walton factory, I was amazed by the scale of production. Rows of machines hummed in perfect rhythm, and the staff showed us how TVs, refrigerators, and smartphones are made. I never realized how much planning and precision goes into each product!
Next, we visited a historic Jamiderbari at Srifaltoli. The intricate architecture and antique furniture took me back in time. I could almost imagine the stories of the families who lived there centuries ago. It was like stepping into a living museum.
I learned that travel doesn't have to be just about sightseeing—sometimes, it's about understanding how things work and appreciating history....
